# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project aims to be useful for engineering, research, education, and simulation automation communities.

Participants should:

- Communicate with technical clarity and respect.
- Share reproducible evidence for bugs and solver behavior.
- Credit relevant Ansys, PyAEDT, MCP, and community sources.
- Keep licensing discussions factual and focused.

## Unacceptable Conduct

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing proprietary models, licenses, credentials, or confidential simulation data.
- Misrepresenting benchmark results or solver outputs.
- Pressuring maintainers for commercial-use permissions inside technical issue threads.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or close issues and pull requests that disrupt the project. Repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.
